Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • All basic stuff for cooking
  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Masalas
  • Curd

Steps

  1. put oil and cook chicken till 40%
  2. then cook rice up to 70%
  3. in a new kadai put a layer of chicken and then a layer of rice, and then a layer of chicken and then rice
  4. like that put all layers and garnish in between layers
    5. \
  5. after that put the lid and cook for atlest 1hour on a medium flame on a tawa for heat distrubution
  6. Serve!
